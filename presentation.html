Nicotine Declining
Blood nicotine from your last puff dropping rapidly. First craving wave may hit within 60 minutes.

Heart Rate Dropping
Cardiovascular system responding to reduced stimulant load. Resting heart rate normalising.
❤️ Cardiovascular
4h
Hand-to-Mouth Urge
The behavioural habit of raising device to mouth peaks. Keep hands busy with water or a fidget.
✋ Behavioural
6h
Cotinine Processing
Liver metabolising nicotine into cotinine. Metabolic clearance accelerating.
🔬 Metabolic
8h
Oxygen Normalising
CO levels decreasing. Oxygen delivery to tissues improving — lungs beginning recovery.
🫁 Lungs
18h
Airway Recovery Begins
Vaping-related airway irritation starting to resolve. Deep breaths feel slightly easier.
🫁 Lungs
36h
Dopamine Crash
Dopamine baseline at its lowest. The flatline feeling is your brain recalibrating — temporary.
📉 Neurological
60h
Taste Buds Waking
Taste receptor recovery accelerating. Food may start tasting noticeably richer.
